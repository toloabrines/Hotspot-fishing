# App Store screenshot requirements

- Store screenshots in `/ios/App/fastlane/screenshots/en-US/`.
- Provide 3 to 10 screenshots for the iPhone App Store listing.
- Export screenshots as PNG or JPG without transparency.
- Capture the main flows: hotspot map, oceanographic layers, forecast detail, and saved fishing spots.
- Include at least one current-generation large-screen iPhone screenshot set before running the `release` lane.
- Review each image for readable labels, no placeholder data, and no personal information.
